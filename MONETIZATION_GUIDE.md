## 🎯 Business Models

### 1. Freemium Model (Recommended)

#### Free Tier
- Basic schedule generation (3 subjects max)
- 1-week schedule view
- Standard break structures
- Community support

#### Pro Tier ($9.99/month)
- Unlimited subjects
- Full semester planning
- Advanced cognitive analysis
- PDF export
- Priority support
- Custom break structures

#### Premium Tier ($19.99/month)
- Everything in Pro
- AI tutor integration
- Progress tracking
- Parent dashboard
- Group study features
- API access

### 2. Usage-Based Pricing

| Feature | Free | Basic ($5/mo) | Pro ($15/mo) |
|---------|------|---------------|--------------|
| Schedules/month | 5 | 50 | Unlimited |
| Subjects | 3 | 10 | Unlimited |
| PDF exports | 0 | 20 | Unlimited |
| AI analysis | Basic | Advanced | Premium |

### 3. Institutional Licensing

#### Schools/Colleges
- **Starter:** $499/year (up to 100 students)
- **Growth:** $1,499/year (up to 500 students)
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing (unlimited)

#### Coaching Centers
- **Per-student:** $2/month
- **Bulk discount:** $1.50/month (100+ students)
- **White-label:** $5,000 setup + $1/month

## 📈 Growth Strategies

### Phase 1: Validation (Months 1-3)
- **Goal:** 1,000 free users
- **Strategy:**
  - Launch on Product Hunt
  - Reddit communities (r/students, r/GetStudying)
  - YouTube influencer partnerships
  - Free for beta testers

### Phase 2: Monetization (Months 4-6)
- **Goal:** 100 paying customers
- **Strategy:**
  - Introduce Pro tier
  - Email marketing to free users
  - Limited-time discounts (50% off first month)
  - Referral program (1 month free)

### Phase 3: Scale (Months 7-12)
- **Goal:** 1,000 paying customers ($10K MRR)
- **Strategy:**
  - Content marketing (study tips blog)
  - SEO optimization
  - Partnership with tutoring platforms
  - Instagram/TikTok student influencers

### Phase 4: Expansion (Year 2)
- **Goal:** $50K MRR
- **Strategy:**
  - International expansion
  - Mobile app launch
  - AI tutor integration
  - Enterprise sales team

## 🛡️ Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks
- **API Costs:** Implement caching, rate limiting
- **Scalability:** Use serverless architecture
- **Data Privacy:** GDPR compliance, encryption

### Market Risks
- **Competition:** Differentiate with AI features
- **Seasonality:** Target different exam cycles globally
- **Free Alternatives:** Premium features & support

### Financial Risks
- **Cash Flow:** Maintain 6-month runway
- **Burn Rate:** Keep team lean initially
- **Pricing:** A/B test pricing tiers
